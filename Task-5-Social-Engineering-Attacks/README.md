# Task 5: Social Engineering Attacks

## Objective
The objective of this task is to understand social engineering attacks, their techniques, impact, and prevention strategies. The task emphasizes how attackers exploit human psychology rather than technical vulnerabilities.

---

## Description
Social engineering attacks manipulate individuals into revealing sensitive information such as passwords, financial details, or confidential organizational data. These attacks rely on deception, trust, fear, and urgency.

This task provides a comprehensive overview of social engineering concepts, common attack methods, real-world examples, and best practices to prevent such attacks.

---

## Topics Covered
- Introduction to Social Engineering  
- What is Social Engineering?  
- Types of Social Engineering Attacks  
  - Phishing  
  - Pretexting  
  - Baiting  
  - Tailgating / Piggybacking  
  - Quizzes and Surveys  
- Impact of Social Engineering Attacks  
- Real-World Case Studies  
- Preventive Measures  
- Challenges in Mitigation
